PhD scholarship in Software Engineering

    A fully funded doctoral scholarship in the field of software engineering is available under the supervision of Dr. Ali Ouni (http://ouniali.github.io/) at the Department of Software Engineering and IT at Ecole de Technologie Superieure (ETS) Montreal, University of Quebec, Canada. The starting date is at the earliest convenience, but preferably no later than January 1, 2019. The applications will be screened continuously and a decision will be made as soon as the right candidate is found.

    The successful candidate will explore the area of software maintenance and evolution and will investigate different techniques from machine learning to optimization and data analytics to maintain and evolve modern software products.

    Ecole de Technologie Superieure (ETS) is located in the heart of Montreal and part of the University of Quebec. Specialized in applied research and teaching in engineering and technology transfer to companies, it teaches engineers and researchers who are recognized for their practical and innovative approach. ETS Montreal is ranked first in Quebec and second in Canada for the total number of engineering diplomas awarded annually.
    Montreal is a vibrant city that has been named the "WORLD’S BEST STUDENT CITY" in a new ranking out February 15, 2017, from #QS.

    Interested applicants are welcome to send their CV, academic transcripts, and a statement of research interests by email to ali.ouni@etsmtl.ca . The email subject should be "Application for PhD scholarship in Software Engineering".
    Further inquiries about the position can be addressed to Dr. Ali Ouni (ali.ouni@etsmtl.ca)
