Call for PhD student position in Software Engineering

    A fully funded doctoral scholarship in the field of software engineering is available under the supervision of Dr. Ali Ouni (http://ouniali.github.io/) at the Department of Software Engineering and IT at Ecole de Technologie Superieure (ETS) Montreal, University of Quebec, Canada. The starting date is at the earliest convenience, but preferably no later than January 1, 2019. The applications will be screened continuously and a decision will be made as soon as the right candidate is found.

    The successful candidate will study the maintenance and evolution of software systems and will explore the area of using optimization and data analytics techniques to support developers in their software engineering activities to better monitor the quality and success of their software products.

    Interested applicants are welcome to send their CV, academic transcripts, and a statement of research interests by email to ali.ouni@etsmtl.ca :

    Further inquiries about the position can be addressed to Dr. Ali Ouni (ali.ouni@etsmtl.ca)
