Call for PhD student position in Software Engineering

    Summary

    A fully funded doctoral scholarship in the field of software engineering is available for excellent students under the supervision of Dr. Ali Ouni (http://ouniali.github.io/) at the Department of Software Engineering and IT at Ecole de Technologie Superieure (ETS) Montreal, University of Quebec, Canada. The appointment is intended to start in September 2018. However, the review of candidates will be performed continuously until the position has been filled.

    The successful candidate is expected to conduct innovative research in the area of using optimization and data analytics techniques to support developers in their software engineering activities to better monitor the quality and success of their software products during software life-cycle. Such activities include software change management, software refactoring, software maintenance and evolution.

    Candidate Profile and Qualifications

    We consider highly motivated candidates who have a Masters degree in software engineering, computer science, or a closely related discipline with excellent grades and/or a distinction at master’s degree. You may apply if you have not completed all requirements for your degree, provided you will have them before the position starts.

    Successful candidates should ideally have a strong interest and background in software engineering, optimization/machine learning, data analytics, and solid programming skills. Applicants will also have to demonstrate an excellent level of spoken and written English, possess good interpersonal and communication skills and show willingness to work as part of an international team. Prior published (or under submission) publications are meritorious.

    ETS Montreal's Environment

    This PhD opportunity will allow the cadidate to performing high quality and timely research in software engineering, optimization and data analytics. A PhD training in this area will provide the candidate with a rich experience combining theoretical and practical aspects and prepare her/him to pursue an academic research and/or industrial career in software engineering.

    Montreal is a vibrant city that has been named the "WORLD’S BEST STUDENT CITY" in a new ranking out February 15, 2017, from #QS. Ecole de Technologie Superieure is located in the heart of Montreal and part of the University of Quebec system. Specialized in applied teaching in engineering and technology transfer to companies, it teaches engineers and researchers who are recognized for their practical and innovative approach. ETS Montreal is ranked first in Quebec and fourth in Canada for the total number of engineering diplomas awarded annually. While ETS is a francophone institution, graduate studies can be done in French or English.

    Interested applicants are requested to submit the following by email to ali.ouni@etsmtl.ca :

    • Curriculum vitae.
    • Academic transcripts and a copy of Master thesis.
    • Contact information of at least two references, including the Master supervisor(s).
